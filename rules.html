Rules & Regulation

ZIPZAP CUP SEASON 1 DATE : 15th December 2023

We are excited to announce the commencement of the ZIP ZAP CUP 2023 Season 1, a one-day soccer tournament open to all. This event will take place at SDI Uttara 6A.

Entry Fee:

To register a team, an entry fee of 3500 BDT is required. To confirm your team's participation, an advance payment of 1000 BDT must be made via BKASH. Please ensure that you include your name in the reference section when making the payment. BKASH Personal Number: 01303570003

Team Composition:

Each team must consist of 6 active players on the field and have 4 additional players as reserves.

Footwear Regulation:

Players are to use turf shoes while on the ground. The use of boots is not permitted, and playing barefoot is generally discouraged. However, in exceptional cases, playing barefoot may be allowed based on the ground conditions.

Code of Conduct:

All participants are expected to maintain a high level of sportsmanship and polite behavior. Any form of non-polite behavior will result in disciplinary action by the organizing committee.

Committee's Authority:

The decisions made by the organizing committee will be final and binding in all matters related to the tournament.

Age Limit:

Players No Limit Extend 20-25 max

Match Rules:

Professional referees will officiate the matches, and offside rules will not be applied. Rolling substitutions will be allowed during the games.
